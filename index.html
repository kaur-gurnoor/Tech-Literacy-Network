What we offer

Three programs, one mission

Each workshop is hands-on, paced for comfort, and completely free. No jargon. No pressure. Just learning.

AI Basics for Everyday Life

Discover what AI actually is, bust the myths, and learn to use tools like ChatGPT confidently and safely — for planning, writing, and daily tasks.

Learn more →
Cyber Safety 101

Spot scams before they spot you. Build strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and take control of your digital privacy — step by step.

Learn more →
Online Kindness Challenge

An empathy-driven workshop that explores cyberbullying, digital respect, and how our words online carry real-world weight.

Learn more →
How it works

Simple from start to finish
