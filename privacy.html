Privacy Policy

How we handle your data.

Last updated: June 2025

Overview

Tech Literacy Network ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website and how we use it. We will never sell your personal data.

Information we collect

Information you give us directly

When you fill out our workshop request form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, organization name, and the details of your request. This information is used solely to respond to your workshop inquiry and is never shared with third parties.

Anonymous analytics data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymous information such as which pages you visit, how long you spend on the site, and what device or browser you use. This data does not identify you personally. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies placed by Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information. By continuing to use our site, you consent to the use of these cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

How we use your information

Information submitted through our contact form is used only to respond to your workshop request. Anonymous analytics data is used to improve our website. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell or share your data with any third parties, except as required by law.

Third-party services

Google Analytics — anonymous website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy · How Google uses data

Formspree — secure form submission handling. Formspree Privacy Policy

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at techliteracynetwork@gmail.com.

Questions? Contact us at techliteracynetwork@gmail.com.
